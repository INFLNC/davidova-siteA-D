— Project

Tastes that don't apologize. A campaign for Vobro's premium praline collection — five flavours, five worlds, one collection that refuses to be quiet.

EGO by Vobro is now competing on shelves across Europe and beyond. The challenge was to lift the visual story above generic premium-praline cliché — gold + brown + flourish — and into a place where each flavour has its own voice, its own world, its own moment. Built entirely in Blender with lifestyle CGI compositions inspired by Sundae Body Mists, Squeeze and modern FMCG editorial.

— Concept

"Five passions. Five worlds. One EGO."

Each EGO praline is a different mood. Hazelnut as restraint, plum as twilight, pistachio as escape, cherry as boldness, almond as lightness. Five flavours that arrive in their own scenes — not in a studio. Pleasure that doesn't apologize.

— HERO SHOT
— Editorial campaign hero
— 01 / Hazelnut Passion

The Stoic

"Found it on the way home."

Milk chocolate shell, smooth hazelnut cream, one whole roasted hazelnut. Forest restraint with a slow finish.

— 02 / Plum Passion

The Mystic

"Rip-it-open o'clock."

Two-layer filling — white cream with cocoa pieces, deep plum reduction. Twilight in a praline.

— 03 / Pistachio Passion

The Adventurer

"Holiday in your pocket."

Vibrant pistachio cream with golden kadayif strands. The Mediterranean inside Polish chocolate.

— 04 / Cherry Passion

The Seducer

"Caught red-handed."

Whole cherry suspended in deep ruby liquid, sealed in milk chocolate. The praline that makes a sound.

— 05 / Almond Passion

The Pure One

"Light enough to float."

White chocolate shell, silky cream, one whole roasted almond. Monochromatic discipline at its sweetest.
