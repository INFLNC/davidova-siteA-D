— Project

A teddy on a blue kitchen morning. Visualization and lifestyle campaign for Vobro's milk chocolate and rice-grain bear range — from pack to playground.

Gryzzzli is Vobro's entry into children's confectionery — a milk chocolate bear with puffed rice grains, sold in flowpack singles and 1 kg stabilopacks. The pack design was finalized by the client; the task was to build a complete visual system around it — shelf-ready CGI, lifestyle scenes and a campaign that speaks to parents and children simultaneously.
