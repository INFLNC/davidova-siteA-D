Project

Reflecting a reflective brand.

Visualization of Delissimo — a three-SKU premium line in two finishes (gold and white). Hazelnut & Almond classic, the gift-bow vertical pack, and the Almond-only edition. The pack design was supplied finalized; the renders cover individual packshots and the lineup as listed on e-commerce.

Concept

The classic SKU's pack is metallic gold throughout — which is the hardest case in product CGI. Too much specular and the artwork disappears; too little, and the pack looks like brown card. The brief was a controlled diffusion that lets the round logo read while keeping the metallic intent.

The white Almond pack was rendered separately with its own lighting setup, then composited back into the lineup at the same exposure as the gold SKUs — so the three packs read as one product family despite the opposite material logic.
