— Project

Abeille Royale is heritage in liquid form — Guerlain's royal jelly serum, a product whose story stretches back through 200 years of French perfumery.

I rebuilt the bottle in Blender as a museum object: gold lit from within, glass faceted like a gem, framed in the chiaroscuro of a vault.

— Concept

"A jewel, not a jar."

Abeille Royale is heritage in liquid form. I built the visual not as cosmetics but as a museum artifact — golden, lit from within, framed in chiaroscuro. The bottle is precious. The honey is its proof.
