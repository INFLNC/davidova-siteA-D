Project

The pack opens twice.

Visualization of the deep-green Hazelnut Passion outer box and individual praline wrappers. The artwork — emerald with gold script, splash composition and whole hazelnut — was provided as a finalized print file. The renders document both the gift-box silhouette and the open-tray view of unit-wrapped pralines.

Concept

Boxed pralines have two faces — the outer pack as bought, and the interior tray as opened. The visualization honours both: a closed-box hero where the gold script does the work, and an opened-lid composition where the foil-wrapped units repeat across a gold tray.

Both versions of the pack — slim vertical and square — are documented side by side, since the same artwork is reused across formats in Vobro's range. The renders preserve the diagonal placement of the wrappers exactly as they sit in the production tray.
