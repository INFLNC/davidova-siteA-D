Project

A teddy on a blue kitchen morning.

Visualization and presentation imagery for Gryzzzli — Vobro's milk chocolate and rice-grain teddy bear range. The brand identity and pack design were finalized by the client; my role was to translate the finished artwork into shelf-ready CGI for catalogue, e-commerce and key-visual use.

Concept

The pack itself sells a clear story — a brown chocolate bear cresting through a wave of milk on a sky-blue field. Rather than copy that scene, the visualization extends it outward: a real breakfast kitchen with the doypack as protagonist, surrounded by the props that already live on the front of the label.

Strawberries, bananas, a glass of milk, granola, a croissant — every supporting object on set is a deliberate echo of an illustration already present on the artwork. The result is a hero image that doesn't just present the pack; it lets the pack walk into its own world.
