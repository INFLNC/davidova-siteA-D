— Project

A perfume of memory, visualized as the silence before a sound. Replica Jazz Club is a study in restraint.

I rebuilt the bottle and its world from scratch in Blender to explore how a fragrance with a clear narrative — late nights, leather, smoke — can be photographed without leaning on its references. The result is a single object, lit like a stage, framed like an editorial.

— Concept

"Memory as material."

Replica is a perfume of memory. I translated that idea into visual language — the warmth of air as the medium of recollection, silence in the composition as the pause before scent.
