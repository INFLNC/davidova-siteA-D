Project

A single object, repeated.

Visualization of Vobro's Cherry Roses — a slim, tall presentation box wrapping a single embossed rose in gold-foil ribbon-script. The pack design (typography, foil stamping, illustration) was delivered final; my work covered the CGI in two registers: editorial detail crop and full lineup.

Concept

The lineup composition treats six identical boxes as a piece of sculpture — vertical, monolithic, slightly fanned. It lets the silhouette become the brand asset before the type does.

The accompanying macro crop holds the camera close to the foil, where the cursive 'Roses' script catches a single direction of light and the burgundy 'Cherry' wordmark stays matte. Two photographs, one object, two reads.
