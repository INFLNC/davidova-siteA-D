— Project

Three flavors spin. One wins. Full CGI campaign for Vobro's marshmallow-jelly chocolate — from pack to playground, from shelf to slot machine.

WOW Pianka is Vobro's play on the children's confectionery market — a marshmallow with jelly in chocolate, sold in three flavors: orange, lemon, cherry. The brief: make something loud enough to stop a child mid-aisle. The answer: a visual system built around casino energy, color explosions, and a cross-section so satisfying it sells itself. Every frame is a slot machine pull — three reels spinning, every result a jackpot.

— Concept

"Pull the lever. Every flavor wins."

The strategy borrows from slot machines and arcade games — bright, fast, rewarding. Each flavor is a color reel: orange pops, lemon glows, cherry pulses. The cross-section macro is the jackpot moment — chocolate shell cracking open to reveal layers of marshmallow and fruit jelly. WOW doesn't whisper. It shouts from the shelf.

— Approach

"Slot machine logic: every pull is a win."

The visual system borrows from arcade culture — saturated colors, fast cuts, reward loops. Every frame is designed to trigger the same dopamine hit as cracking open the chocolate itself. Loud, bright, unapologetic.
