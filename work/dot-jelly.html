Project

The pack at the eye of the splash.

Visualization of Dot Jelly — Vobro's pectin fruit jelly range in orange, cherry and lemon. The pack artwork (three dot graphics, white doypack body, flavour callouts) was supplied finished. The CGI builds the splash environment around it: real citrus halves, cherries, juice spray and back-light.

Concept

The three flavour 'dot' graphics on the artwork already imply motion — the rendered scene continues that motion outward. Orange halves rotate in from one side, cherries cluster behind, a lemon wedge floats in juice-mist on the other.

Every fruit in the scene matches a flavour 'dot' on the artwork — citrus for orange, ruby for cherry, lime-green for lemon. The pack is photographed front-on, perfectly clean, with the chaos arranged around its edge.
