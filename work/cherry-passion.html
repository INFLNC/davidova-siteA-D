Project

The same grammar, different temperature.

Cherry Passion is the magenta-pink companion to Hazelnut Passion in Vobro's Passion line. The pack artwork — pink gradient, gold ribbon, cherry-and-chocolate splash — was supplied final. The CGI mirrors the Hazelnut workflow: open-tray hero with hero plaque, plus two closed-pack views.

Concept

Where Hazelnut Passion was cool and forest-toned, Cherry Passion sits at the opposite end of the heat-map. The visualization carries the same lighting setup and tray geometry, so the two SKUs read as a coherent line when displayed beside each other on shelf or in catalogue.

Switching the background to a moody gold-particle field reinforces the gift-context that the magenta artwork already implies — without competing with the splash composition printed on the plaque.
